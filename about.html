About

Infinite Studios is a commercial recording facility in the greater Bay Area owned by acclaimed engineer & producer Michael Denten. On the cutting edge of modern day technology, Infinite boasts a state of the art Avid S6 mixing console as well as the coveted Burl Audio Mothership, making it one of the premiere and highest quality studios in Northern California.

Mixing and Mastering have quickly become an in-house specialty as Michael continues to amass an impressive list of credits. Clients include E-40, Tupac Shakur, MC Hammer, Walter Hawkins, Bobby Brown & Giovanni Hidalgo, as well as single credits that include the timeless classic "I Got 5 On It." The studio is also fully equipped with surround 5.1 mixing capabilities that has led to work on numerous films / TV shows including Hawaii 5-0 and the re-release of the Rocky V soundtrack.

For more information or quotes email info@infinitestudios.com

With more Gold & Platinum records than a dozen people can count on their hands, we are the Bay Area's premier provider for sound engineering.
